SP GlowPetals — Born from Love

SP GlowPetals was founded with a simple but powerful belief — that every gift should carry a piece of the sender's heart. We started as a small gifting studio and grew into a beloved brand trusted by thousands across India.

Our curated collections of candles, flowers, surprise boxes, and personalized gifts are designed to make every occasion unforgettable — from birthdays and anniversaries to corporate appreciation and festivals.
